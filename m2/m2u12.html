Microservices and Microkernels Debate:

In my opinion I think that microkernel- based operating systems are better than those that rely on microservices. This is down to one aspect, security. In Biggs et al (2018) there are many positives of microkernel-based operating systems presented. For example, the authors (Biggs et al. 2018) conducted a study analyzing 115 critical Linux vulnerabilities (CVEs) to assess how they would be affected by a microkernel-based OS design. They found that 96% of critical Linux exploits would no longer be critical in severity in a microkernel-based system.
If this is compared to the microservices-based operating systems, it seems the positives are nowhere near as significant in my opinion. For example, in Bucchiarone et al (2018) some of the main positives of microservices presented are: improved scalability, easier deployment and flexibility to name a few. Although these are significant within the article itself it points out that there are trade-offs with microservices.
Overall, for me I believe that microkernel based is better simply due to security it offers.
